Who: Honda Hawk GT

Hi, I'm a Honda Hawk GT - the RC31 model. Toshiaki Kishi, my designer, made me one of the first modern naked bikes. Some say I am always in my birthday suit. If you're on the hunt for your first motorcycle, look no further - well, it might require a hard look; I'm a rare bird to find.

What: My heart

I have been told the sound of my beating heart can sound like a lion's roar. My engine may not be like others, but it can still get you into trouble if there is no respect towards the throttle. There are old riders and there are bold riders - my heart does not always allow for old, bold riders.

When: Produced from 1988 to 1991

My model was in production from 1988 to 1991. I may have been produced for a longer time, but the market, at that point in time, was not interested in motorcycles like me. It has taken almost half a decade for any sort of interest in my sort of look, but now there is so much interest that I can be hard to find.

Where: Places I can go.

My power may shine on long, straight empty roads, but my handling shines better on curvy, country roads. If you're on the interstate and you desire to get ahead of a pack of cars, no need to downshift - just open the throttle a bit more. If you're confident with your vestibular sense, then you can test my limits at a road racing course.
